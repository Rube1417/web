ABOUT

Rosales National High School is a public secondary school located along Coloma Street in Don Antonio Village, Rosales, Pangasinan, Philippines. Established as a national high school through Republic Act No. 4325 on June 19, 1965, the school has long been a cornerstone of education in the community. It offers junior and senior high school education, serving students from Rosales and nearby barangays. Beyond academics, the school is known for its active student life, including intramurals, clubs, and various school events that help students grow not only intellectually but also socially. For many families in Rosales, Rosales National High School is more than just a school it is a place where friendships are built, talents are discovered, and dreams begin to take shape.
