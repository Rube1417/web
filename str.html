STRANDS

🧮 STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics)

STEM is for students who enjoy solving problems, experimenting, and working with numbers. This strand focuses on advanced Math and Science subjects like Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Calculus. It prepares students for college courses such as Engineering, Medicine, Architecture, IT, and other science-related fields.

📖 HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences)

HUMSS is ideal for students who love reading, writing, speaking, and understanding society. It focuses on subjects like Politics, Psychology, Literature, and Communication. This strand prepares students for careers in Law, Education, Journalism, Social Work, and Public Service.

💼 ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management)

ABM is for future entrepreneurs and business leaders. Students learn about business operations, marketing, accounting, and finance. This strand is a good foundation for courses like Business Administration, Accountancy, Marketing, and Entrepreneurship.

🧠 GAS (General Academic Strand)

GAS is perfect for students who are still exploring their interests. It offers a mix of subjects from different strands, giving students flexibility while helping them prepare for various college courses.

🛠️ TECHNICAL-VOCATIONAL-LIVELIHOOD (TVL) TRACK

The TVL track focuses on practical skills and hands-on training. Students can earn certifications that help them work right after senior high school. Common TVL specializations may include: Cookery Bread and Pastry Production Information and Communications Technology (ICT) Electrical Installation and Maintenance Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW) Home Economics-related courses This track is great for students who prefer skills-based learning and want job-ready training.
